Challenge

Ensuring healthy lives and promoting well-being for all at all ages is essential to sustainable development, and is United Nations sustainable development goal 3.

About seven million people die worldwide every year from air pollution, and 91% of the world population breathe air that contains high levels of pollutants. WHO has called air pollution "a silent public health emergency".

There are over one million confirmed deaths worldwide from covid-19, many suffer lasting health effects and the economic and social impacts are devastating. Disruptions caused by Covid-19 could reverse decades of progress in healthcare worldwide.

Solution

Introducing the pollution detecting bracelet that also keeps you updated on the current virus situation near you. Our goal is to maximize awareness while minimizing exposure to dangerous pollutants and infectious viruses.
