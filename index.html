Pollutant detecting device

for instant exposure awareness

Challenge

About seven million people die worldwide every year from air pollution, and 91% of the world population breathe air that contains high levels of pollutants [1]. WHO has called air pollution "a silent public health emergency" [2]. There are over one million confirmed deaths worldwide from covid-19, many suffer lasting health effects and the economic and social impacts are devastating [3]. Disruptions caused by Covid-19 could reverse decades of progress in healthcare worldwide [4]. We hope that Expozance can contribute to solving some of these problems and give better health and well-being for its users, which is essential to sustainable development and is one of United Nations sustainable development goals.

Solution

Introducing the pollution detecting bracelet that also keeps you updated on the current virus situation near you. Our goal is to maximize awareness while minimizing exposure to dangerous pollutants and infectious viruses.
