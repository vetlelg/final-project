New idea development: Virus updates.
As real-time virus detection is not yet possible, we want the device to keep people updated on the corona situation where they are. The device will note your location and get information about the spread of the virus and danger of getting exposed from statistics provided by Norwegian Institute of Public Health (NIPH), Norwegian Directorate of Health and the Minister of Health.
