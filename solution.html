Air pollution detection

As real-time virus detection is not yet possible, we still want to keep you updated during these times of the coronavirus. We understand the continuing stream of constantly changing information can be tedious, which makes the importance of getting updated information easily imperative. The device will use your location and get information about the risk of exposure from statistics provided by ->the Norwegian Institute of Public Health (NIPH). These statistics will be updated daily and shows number of reported cases in the county and municipality where you are located at the moment.
